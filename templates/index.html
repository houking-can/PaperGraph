Construct graph based on a scientific paper

Scigraph is a data mining framework, the input is scientific literature in PDF format. Scigraph extracts important information about the literature, including metadata, text, and forms. In addition, Scigraph identifies the Paper, Method, Problem, Limitation entities etc., then it extracts the relationships between entities, and constructs the knowledge graph of a specific paper. After knowledge fusion, we can construct academic domain knowledge graph in the future.
